About

Posted on Sep 16, 2020

Hello, my name is Adrian Ordonez. I am a Software Engineer turned DevOps Engineer. I grew up learning about computers from my IT Director father. Computers and technology has become my biggest passion and hobby and I feel amazingly lucky to have my biggest passion as my day job.

Just generally anything that catches my eye at the time.

If you’re interested in interacting, checkout my Twitter and my GitHub. This blog is the first step into me being much more active on social media and interacting with the community and I cannot wait to meet new people and learn more!
